Home · Our Story

It started with a family table in Italy

"Mangiamo" is the word an Italian family says when the food is ready and everyone gathers around. That spirit of family, generosity, and doing things the real way is the whole reason we exist.

The Dream

From construction sites to a caffè by the sea

For more than 30 years, our founder built things with his hands in construction. But he was always a son of Italy, with family still living in Northern Italy and a heart full of memories from summers spent there.

On vacation after vacation, he and his wife fell in love all over again with the country, the culture, and, of course, the gelato. He'd talk about it constantly: the little gelaterias, the flavors, the way a simple scoop could stop you in your tracks.

One day, they tasted gelato made fresh, right in the shop. It was a revelation. That was the moment a dream became a decision.

"If you're going to risk your career and invest your retirement, it better be for something good."

Our Journey

A family business, one scoop at a time

The Inspiration

Summers in Northern Italy

Family roots in Northern Italy, vacations spent falling in love with Italian culture, and discovering that real gelato is nothing like what's sold frozen in the States.

2009

Mangiamo opens its doors

After 30+ years in construction, the family bet everything on a dream: bring authentic, fresh-made Italian gelato to Orange County. The promise: never warehouse-frozen, always made fresh in-store.

The Craft

Trained the Italian way

An Italian gelato expert taught the team to make gelato and sorbetto from scratch: small batches, real ingredients, naturally colored (our pistachio is muted green, never neon).

Today

On Main Street, a block from the pier

Owner Nick makes the gelato fresh every day and greets guests by name. "Grazie Nick!" is in hundreds of reviews. We're proud to be the family gelateria of Surf City USA.

Why Families Choose Us

The Mangiamo promise

🇮🇹 Authentic

Recipes and technique brought from Italy, by a family that learned it where it was born, not from a corporate manual.

🍦 Always Fresh

Made in small batches in our shop, every day. Never made in a warehouse, frozen and shipped like most American "gelato."

❤️ Personal

A real person makes your gelato and remembers your name. We treat every guest the way we'd treat family at our own table.
