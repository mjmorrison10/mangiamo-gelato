Good questions, real answers

A little about gelato, our shop, and visiting us by the pier.

What's the difference between gelato and ice cream?
Gelato is the Italian cousin of ice cream — but it's made differently. It has a lower fat content (more milk, less cream) and far less air is churned into it. That makes gelato denser, silkier, and more intensely flavored than ice cream, and it's served slightly warmer so it's beautifully soft on the spoon.
Where does gelato come from?
Gelato is a beloved Italian frozen dessert with roots reaching back to ancient Rome and Egypt, where people mixed snow and ice with fruit, juices and honey. The modern version is credited to Renaissance Florence — legend says the architect Bernardo Buontalenti created it in the 16th century for a grand feast. We bring that centuries-old tradition to Main Street.
Is your gelato really made fresh in the shop?
Yes — and it's the whole reason we exist. Most "gelato" in the U.S. is made in a warehouse, frozen, and shipped to stores. Ours is made fresh in small batches in our shop, every day, with real, natural ingredients. (That's why our pistachio is a natural muted green, not neon.)
Do you have dairy-free or vegan options?
Absolutely. Our fruit sorbetti — strawberry, mango, lemon, raspberry champagne and more — are made with real fruit and no cream, so they're naturally dairy-free and vegan-friendly. Just ask which sorbetti are on today.
What else do you serve besides gelato?
We're a true Italian caffè: LaVazza espresso & coffee drinks (hot and iced), the signature affogato (espresso over gelato), plus açaí bowls and fresh smoothies. There's something here all day long.
Where are you, and how close is the pier?
We're at 122 Main St, Ste 101, Huntington Beach, CA 92648 — right on Main Street, just one block from the Huntington Beach Pier. Walk up Main from the pier and you'll find us on the way. See directions & map →
Where do I park?
There's metered street parking right out front on Main Street, plus a public parking garage around the corner. It's an easy stop whether you're heading to or from the beach.

We're open Tuesday–Sunday, 11 AM – 9 PM, and closed Mondays. Hours can shift seasonally, so check our Visit page or call ahead.
Do you cater events?
Yes! We cater weddings, baby showers, birthdays and corporate events across Orange County — gelato carts, pre-packed cups, bulk tubs and custom flavors. Request a catering quote →
Can I get a taste before I choose?
Always. We want you to taste before you commit — just ask. Picking is the hardest (and best) part.
