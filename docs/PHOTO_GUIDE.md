# 📸 Mangiamo Gelato Caffe — Photo Guide

A simple checklist for which real photos to provide and where each one goes on the
website. Real photos are the single highest-impact upgrade left: they prove the
"authentic, fresh, family-made" story that franchises like Baskin-Robbins and
Handel's can't fake, and fresh photos also boost your Google/Maps ranking near the
pier.

---

## ⚠️ Important: only use photos you own
Photos that **customers** uploaded to Yelp or Google belong to those customers, not
to the business — reusing them on your website is a copyright risk. Use only:
- Photos **you or your staff/family took**, or
- Photos you have **written permission** to use.

Good news: many of your own best listing photos are likely ones **you uploaded**,
and anything new you shoot is 100% yours.
- **Google Maps:** your profile → Contributions → Photos (download your own).
- **Yelp:** your profile → Photos (download your own).

## 🛠️ Photo specs (so they look great on the site)
- **Highest resolution you have** — we can downscale, but we can't upscale.
  Aim for **2000px+ on the long edge**.
- **Orientation:** hero & banners = **landscape/horizontal**; flavor cards = square or
  portrait is fine.
- **Bright and in-focus** beats artsy-and-dark for food. Natural daylight is ideal.
- **Format:** JPG or PNG. Don't worry about file size — we optimize everything for the web.
