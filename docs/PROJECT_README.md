## 🎯 The strategic problem we solved
You see Baskin-Robbins cups on the pier, not Mangiamo's. Root causes we found:
1. **Geography:** Handel's is *on* the pier corner; Baskin-Robbins is *between* you and the pier. Walkers get intercepted before reaching you a block up.
2. **No walking billboard:** competitors' bright cups/spoons advertise themselves; your plain white cup is invisible.
3. **Muted storefront** vs. loud national palettes = low crave-signal.
4. **A thin website with no SEO/ordering** = you lose the "gelato near the pier" Google search competitors win.

**We can't move your store — so we win the *digital* pier corner** (search, maps, ordering, story) and convert deciders before they settle for a franchise.
