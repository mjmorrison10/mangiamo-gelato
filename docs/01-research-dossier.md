### Why Mangiamo loses the pier-walk battle (root causes)
1. **Geography of interception:** Handel's sits *on* the pier corner; Baskin-Robbins sits *between* Mangiamo and the pier. Walkers are captured before they reach Mangiamo. Mangiamo is "a block away" = past the decision point.
2. **Portability/visibility:** competitors' cups/spoons are bright and brand-loud; Mangiamo's white cup is invisible on the boardwalk. **No walking advertisement.**
3. **Signage & crave-signal:** muted gray/navy storefront vs. bright national palettes. Doesn't shout "ICE CREAM" to a hot, distracted tourist.
4. **Brand recall:** Baskin-Robbins & Handel's have instant national/award recognition; Mangiamo relies on word-of-mouth.
5. **Digital discoverability:** thin website + no SEO/ordering = loses the "gelato near me / dessert near Huntington Beach pier" search, which competitors capture.

### What competitors do that we should adopt
- **Signature recurring promo** (Handel's "Two Buck Tuesday"; BR value scoops) → drives habit & word of mouth.
- **Daily-flavor social posts** (Handel's posts daily flavors; we'll surface a live "Today's Flavors" on site).
- **Abundance & quality signaling** ("#1 in the world", huge scoops) — Mangiamo's equivalent = **"Made fresh today, like in Italy."**
- **Photogenic, shareable presentation** (IceCreamTon churros) — Mangiamo should style cones/affogato for the camera.
- **Prime visual interception** — we can't move the store, but we can win the **digital pier corner** (search + maps + "order ahead / find us") and create a portable brand cue.

## 9. STRATEGIC BRIEF FOR THE WEBSITE (what it must do)
**Positioning:** *"Authentic Italian gelato, made fresh every day, a block from the pier — by a family that learned it in Italy."*
**Primary goals (in order):**
1. Win local search ("gelato Huntington Beach / near pier") via SEO + LocalBusiness schema → capture pier-bound deciders BEFORE they hit BR/Handel's.
2. Convert visitors with crave-first imagery + clear CTAs: **Get Directions**, **See the Flavors**, **Order Online**, **Cater Your Event**.
3. Tell the **family story** to build the emotional, premium, "real Italy" preference over franchises.
4. Generate **catering leads** (dedicated page + form).
5. Drive **repeat visits** with a signature promo + email/IG capture.
**Brand feel:** warm Italian (deep navy from current wordmark + warm terracotta/pistachio/cream + gold accents), appetizing photography, family-told voice, modern but timeless. Loud enough to crave, classy enough to feel authentic.
