Why It Tastes Different

Most "gelato" is shipped frozen.
Ours is born here.

Across America, most gelato is made in a warehouse, frozen, and trucked to the store. You can taste the difference. We can't.

That's exactly why we opened. Every flavor at Mangiamo is made fresh, in small batches, right here on Main Street. It's denser, smoother, and more intense than ice cream, with real, natural ingredients. Our pistachio is naturally muted green, not neon. Our strawberry sorbetto is dairy-free and bright as a Sicilian afternoon.

"If you're going to do it, do it for something good." The Mangiamo family.
